About Our School

Nurturing young minds with love, care, and education.

Welcome to Mothers Pride Playschool

Established with a vision to redefine early childhood education, Mothers Pride Playschool is more than just a play school. It is a second home for your child.

We believe that every child is unique and deserves an environment that celebrates their individuality. Our curriculum is specifically designed keeping in mind the cognitive, physical, and emotional development of a child.

Through games, interactive storytelling, 3D illustrative learning, and our highly experienced staff, we make learning a joyful adventure!

Our Vision

To foster an inclusive environment where young learners can discover their passions, build fundamental skills, and develop a lifelong love for learning and discovery.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality early childhood education through an innovative play-way methodology, ensuring cognitive growth, social responsibility, and strong family partnerships.

Why Choose Us?

We combine a highly active learning curriculum with top-of-the-line infrastructure. Our educators act as facilitators, guiding children as they explore the world around them.
